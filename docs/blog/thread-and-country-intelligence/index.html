Thread Intelligence and Country Intelligence: Track How Stories Move

March 22, 2026  ·  Global Perspectives

Headlines tell you what happened today. They do not tell you how the story got here — or where it is going.

That gap is the reason Global Perspectives exists. And it is why we built Thread Intelligence and Country Intelligence: two new features that turn today's news into a structured narrative you can actually act on.

Thread Intelligence: the story behind the story

Every topic on Global Perspectives is linked to a persistent narrative thread — a through-line that connects related events across days and geographies. Thread Intelligence surfaces that through-line explicitly.

For each of the top 10 active story arcs, Thread Intelligence generates:

  • Story arc — how the narrative has developed from its origin to today
  • Trajectory forecast — where the story is most likely to go next, and why
  • Root cause chain — the structural forces and prior events that produced this situation
  • Watch questions — the specific signals that will indicate how the story breaks

Thread Intelligence is updated daily. It is available to signed-in users at the Member tier — free during the current launch period.

The thread system itself has been running since launch: every topic carries a threadId that links it to earlier and later coverage of the same story. Thread Intelligence is the first feature that makes that underlying structure directly visible and readable.

Country Intelligence: a daily briefing for the world's most active countries

Understanding a country's situation means understanding the full set of stories unfolding there simultaneously — not just the loudest headline of the day.

Country Intelligence generates a daily briefing for the top 10 most active countries, covering:

  • Situation summary — what is actually happening across all active threads
  • Cross-thread insight — how different stories interact and compound each other
  • Trajectory — the direction the country's overall situation is moving
  • Risk signals — specific indicators worth watching

Country briefings draw on the same thread analyses and article coverage that powers the rest of the platform. The AI reads across active story arcs and recent news simultaneously — producing a country-level view that no individual thread provides on its own.

Country Intelligence is accessible from the Weekly view. Select any active country to see its full briefing, active story arcs, and day-by-day coverage.

Why this matters for analysts and researchers

A consultant preparing a client risk assessment can now use Country Intelligence to build a structured picture of a country's situation in under an hour — with source-traceable AI analysis, not just links to aggregate. A researcher tracking how coverage of a conflict has shifted can use Thread Intelligence to follow the exact narrative evolution across sources over weeks.

These are not convenience features. They are the difference between reading news and understanding it.

What's included at each tier

  • Free (no account required) — daily topics, AI summaries, world map
  • Member ($15/month) — 7-day archive, narrative threads, Thread Intelligence, Country Intelligence, weekly map
  • Enterprise — 30-day archive, custom requirements — contact us

During the current launch period, all signed-in users have full Member access at no cost.
